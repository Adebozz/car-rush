About us

DOYSE EDUCATIONAL CONSULTS is an educational consulting firm in Nigeria offering study abroad placement opportunities to prospective students.
We have a global placement opportunities that our students can benefit from. This makes securing admission and processing their visa application
an easy ride. Students are able to count on our professionalism and expertise in this area.

We are able to render expert advice to all our students without bias. We have partner institutions ranging from Colleges to Universities,
offering diverse range of programs from certificates, diploma, associate degrees, Graduate Certificate, Graduate Diploma, Master (Coursework), Master
(Research) and Doctorate programs. We also assist accommodation for our students with the help of our partner institutions.

Students can rely on our expertise in assisting them in counseling and packaging them for their visa application. Without a valid study permit or visa,
an international student may not be able to enrol and study his or her program. Therefore, this is very crucial that it is properly done to meet the requirements
as stated by various embassies and High Commissions. Based on our experience in the industry, clients can expect an excellent service from us.
